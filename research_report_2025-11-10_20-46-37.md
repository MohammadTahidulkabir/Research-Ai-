### [1] A Metamorphic Testing Perspective on Knowledge Distillation for Language Models of Code: Does the Student Deeply Mimic the Teacher?

**Published:** 2025-11-07 | **arXiv:** [2511.05476v1](http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.05476v1)
**Categories:** cs.SE, cs.LG

**Summary:**
This research paper proposes MetaCompress, a metamorphic testing framework that evaluates the behavioral fidelity of compressed language models of code by comparing their outputs under behavior-preserving metamorphic relations. The framework utilizes three knowledge distillation techniques (Compressor, AVATAR, and MORPH) to compress large language models, and then applies metamorphic testing to identify discrepancies in the predictive behavior of the teacher and student models.
